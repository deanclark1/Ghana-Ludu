About Ghana Ludu

Ludu is one of Ghana's most loved board games. A mix of luck, strategy and pure vibes. Whether you're sending your opponent “home” or praying for a 6 to start or to kick your opponent, the chaos never ends. This version brings the same energy to your browser. I built this with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Upcoming Features

I'll be adding more of the Ludu wickedness soon, including:

  • Forward Kick: Land on an opponent while moving forward to kick them out
  • Back Kick: Land on an opponent counting backward to kick them out
  • Side kick: Land on a parallel cell with opponent to send them home
  • Home kick: Land on an opponent on their way home to kick them out
  • Implement stacking rule (2+ tokens block path)
  • Choose which token to move after rolling
  • Add animation when sending a token home
  • Allow choosing number of players
  • Add sound effects for dice rolls and kicks
  • Add an AI opponent (play with computer)
  • Online multiplayer (play with friends)
